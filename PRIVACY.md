# Privacy Policy

**FadCam** is a fully offline dashcam application. We take your privacy seriously.

## Data Collection

**FadCam collects absolutely nothing.**

- ❌ No analytics
- ❌ No crash reporting
- ❌ No telemetry
- ❌ No advertising SDKs
- ❌ No third-party trackers
- ❌ No network requests (except optional user-initiated media sharing to the Photos library)
- ❌ No user accounts
- ❌ No cloud storage

## What Stays on Your Device

All recordings, photos, settings, and metadata remain exclusively on your device. FadCam never transmits any data over the internet. The app stores everything locally in your device's Documents directory.

## Permissions

- **Camera** — used only to record video. No video frames leave your device.
- **Microphone** — used only to capture audio alongside video recordings.
- **Photo Library** — accessed only when you explicitly choose to save a recording or photo.
